PSC kya hota hai? (Short Explanation)

PSC (Public Service Commission) ek constitutional ya statutory body hoti hai jo government ke liye officers & higher level posts ka selection process handle karti hai. Central level par UPSC hai, jabki har state ke liye alag‚Äìalag State PSC hota hai.

  • Written exam + interview + document verification
  • Reservation & eligibility government rules ke according
  • Final recommendation state/central government ko bheja jata hai

Who should follow PSC exams?

FAQ ‚Äì PSC Related Common Doubts

PSC aur SSC me kya difference hai?
PSC generally Group A & B (officer level) posts ke liye responsible hota hai, jabki SSC / state subordinate boards mostly clerical, assistant, constable, group C level posts ke liye exams conduct karte hain. Yeh state‚Äìto‚Äìstate thoda vary bhi kar sakta hai.
Ek hi candidate multiple PSC exams de sakta hai?
Haan, agar aap eligibility condition (age limit, qualification, domicile etc.) fulfill karte hain to aap multiple states ke PSC exams me apply kar sakte hain. Bas notification me di gayi conditions dhyan se padna zaroori hai.
