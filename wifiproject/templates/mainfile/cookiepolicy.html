Cookie Policy | How WifiResult Uses Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your overall experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core features like page navigation and basic security. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.
  • Performance & Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., most visited pages, time on page) so we can improve content and navigation.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (such as language, last viewed pages) to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising/Third-Party: When enabled, these may be used by our partners to deliver relevant ads and measure the effectiveness of campaigns.

Types of Cookies We Set

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close the browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until deleted.
  • First-party cookies: Set by wifiresult.com to support site functionality and analytics.
  • Third-party cookies: Set by external services integrated on our site (e.g., analytics, embedded media). These are governed by the respective providers’ policies.

Third-Party Cookies & Services

We may use trusted third-party services (for example, analytics or embedded content providers) that set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies directly. Please review the respective providers’ cookie or privacy policies for more information.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking certain cookies may affect site functionality.

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions
  • Safari (macOS): Preferences > Privacy
  • Android/iOS browsers: Refer to in-app privacy/cookie controls

If we implement a cookie banner or preference center, you will be able to update your consent choices directly from that interface.

Consent

By continuing to use wifiresult.com, you consent to the placement of cookies on your device as described in this policy. Where legally required, we will request your explicit consent through a cookie banner.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Effective Date”.

Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy, reach us at wifiresult3@gmail.com. Please include relevant details such as page URL and your browser/device info if reporting a cookie issue.
