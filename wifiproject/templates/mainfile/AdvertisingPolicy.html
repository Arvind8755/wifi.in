Advertising Policy – Transparency & Sponsorship Guidelines

Purpose

At WifiResult, ads help us keep the service free while maintaining high standards of accuracy for government job updates. This Advertising Policy explains how we display ads, how they are labelled, what we do not allow, and how we comply with Google AdSense and industry standards.

Ad Labelling & Separation

  • All advertisements are clearly labelled as “Ad”, “Advertisement”, or “Sponsored”.
  • Ads are visually separated from editorial content via spacing and/or borders so users can distinguish them easily.
  • We do not place ads within headlines or in ways that mislead users to click.

Placement & Density

  • We follow the Better Ads Standards (no intrusive pop-ups that block content, no auto-playing audio ads).
  • Ad density is controlled to keep a good reading experience; we avoid excessive ads above the fold.
  • Clickable areas are not disguised as content. We never encourage accidental clicks (“Click here to proceed”, deceptive arrows, etc.).

Prohibited Content (Zero-Tolerance)

  • No ads related to illegal activities, adult content, hate speech, or deceptive financial schemes.
  • No malware, forced downloads, or ads that mimic system/antivirus warnings.
  • No ads that lead to pages with excessive, disruptive, or misleading experiences.

Third-Party Networks & Cookies

  • We may use third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense to serve ads.
  • These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to show more relevant ads. Users can manage preferences using the Google Ads Settings and browser controls.
  • Our use of cookies and personal data is covered in our Privacy Policy.

Children’s Policy

  • WifiResult is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly target ads to children under 13 years of age.
  • Where applicable, we implement limited ads or disable personalised ads to comply with children’s privacy laws (e.g., COPPA-like principles).

Affiliate & Sponsored Disclosures

  • From time to time, we may include affiliate links or sponsored placements. Such content is clearly disclosed as “Sponsored” or “Affiliate”.
  • Editorial independence is maintained — advertising does not influence our recruitment coverage or conclusions.

User Privacy & Data

  • We minimise data collection for ad serving and comply with applicable privacy laws.
  • Users may opt-out of personalised ads via ad settings or consent tools (where implemented).
  • See our Privacy Policy for details on data, retention and user rights.

Policy Compliance & Reporting Bad Ads

  • We continuously monitor ad placements and performance for policy compliance.
  • If you encounter a misleading or harmful advertisement, please report it with a screenshot and URL.
  • Report to: wifiresult3@gmail.com (Subject: “Ad Complaint”).

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Advertising Policy to reflect updates in Google AdSense requirements or industry standards. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
